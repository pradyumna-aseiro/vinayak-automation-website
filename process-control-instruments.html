Sapcon Instruments Pvt Ltd make level instruments (Switches)for measuring liquids ,Solid And powders. This can be used in various industries like Cement, Packaging, power, Drugs& Pharmaceuticals, Cosmetic,Chemical,Food, Fertilizer Industries,Etc.

VITAL- Vibrating Fork Level Sensor/Switch for Solids

Compact vibrating fork level switch for free-flowing solids, granules and powders

  • Application Temperature Range : up to 200° C
  • Fast Switching: 0.5s , 0.8s options available
  • Calibration Free, independent of material's dielectric properties
  • No moving parts, longer running life
  • Outputs: DPDT Relay, Open Collector PNP Output
  • Certifications: Flame Proof Housing IIC, IP-68 Housing


ELIXIR- Tuning Fork Level Sensor/Switch for Liquids

Elixir-Liquid Level Switch for sticky, corrosive, agitated, splashing, foaming liquids, suitable for liquids with viscosity up to 10,000 cp

  • Application Temperature Range : up to 200° C
  • Calibration Free, independent of material's dielectric properties
  • Output: 8.2V Namur, SPDT & DPDT Relay type current output
  • ECTFE coated, polished & hygienic fork
  • Threads, flanges and hygienic connection (Tri-Clamp)
  • Extendable probe length from 68 to 3000 mm
  • Certifications: Flame Proof Housing IIC, PESO, BIS, CCOE, EIL

Elixir-T-Uni: Vibrating Fork Type Level Sensor for Liquids in Hygienic Applications

Elixir-T-Uni-Compact Tubular Vibrating Fork Level Limit Switch (suitable for all kinds of liquids having viscosity up to 10,000 cp & density greater than or equal to 0.7gm/cc)

  • Application Temperature Range : Up to 150° C
  • Certified EL Class I by EHEDG
  • Enclosure : IP68 Weather Proof Housing
  • Process connections: Threads and hygienic connection (Tri-Clamp)
  • Wetted parts: SS 316, SS 316L with Hygienic finish (Ra < 0.8µ)
  • Compact probe length 68 mm, 126 mm & up to 1000 mm

Coat-Endure: RF-Admittance Level Switch/Sensor for Stick Solids and Liquids

Compact Admittance Level Limit switch for use in sticky solids, pastes and slurries where dielectric constant K > 2.5

  • Application Temperature Range : up to 250° C
  • Immune to application material build-up/coating
  • Suitable for conductive and non-conductive application media
  • Wetted Parts PTFE, SS 304 & SS 316
  • Outputs: DPDT Relay, Open Collector PNP Output
  • Probe length : 65 mm to 1500 mm
  • Certifications : Flame Proof IIC & IP68 Housing


Capvel-FUEL: Level Sensor/Transmitter for remote tracking of fuel

Fuel Level Sensor (use for vehicle tracking systems and DG sets)

  • Application Temperature Range : 20 to 70°C
  • In-built level to volume conversion
  • Application guided installation and calibration
  • Light in weight
  • Wetted Parts: PTFE, Delrin & Aluminium
  • Measurement accuracy : +/­ 1% FSL
  • Response time : 1 to 5s (dynamic)
  • Process connections : SAE 5 mounting holes & 1″ BSP thread
  • Probe length : 100 mm to 1500 mm,
  • Certifications : Flame Proof IIC & IP68 Housing

Vibrosonde: Vibrating Rod Type Level Sensor/Switch for Solids

Vibrating Rod Level Limit Switch (use in powders, sand, sugar, Pulverized coal, Food grains, Cement, Granular material)

  • Application Temperature Range : up to 200° C
  • Calibration Free, independent of material's Di-electric Properties
  • Wetted parts: SS 316
  • Probe Length: 255 mm to 3000 mm
  • Outputs: DPDT Relay, Open Collector
  • Certifications: Flame Proof IIC, IP68 Housing
