Welcome To Vinayak Automation Products

We take immense pleasure to introduce ourselves as Vinayak Automation Products(VAP), deals in Automation Products & do custom need Factory automation, System Integration for your requirement.
VAP established in 2007 as a distributor of Jayashree Products, CG-Emotron Drives & Dynaflux AC DC Motors, & provider of wide range of world class sensor solution, Level measuring products for Solid & Liquid, Flow measurement of water (Flow Meter). Apart from these we take up design , development, sourcing of any turnkey base requirement for all types of Industrial Projects. We give our services for Integration Projects for various Industries with PLC, HMI, SCADA, VFD, Control Panels,and Servo Motors & Drives. The Company has earned immense credibility and a strong clientele consisting of reputed OEMs and EU. Our ability to deliver demonstrated results to our entire customer has resulted in our Rapid Growth and Industry Reputation.
Within a span of 8 years we have catered around 5000 dedicated clientele in different industrial sector.

We provide our Product services & Engineering Solutions to following Sectors:

Automobile, Cement, Coal, Ceramics, Steel, Sponge Iron, Foundry, Factory Automation, Power Generation, Rubber, Textile, Paper, Solar Pumping, Sugar, healthcare, Oil and Gas, Pharmaceutical, Chemical & Bulk Drugs, Food and Beverages, Packaging, Printing, Construction, Elevators and Escalators, Building Automation, Pumping Stations, & General Industries.

We have hired quality control professionals to make sure that the finest quality is maintained throughout the fabrication and distribution departments.
